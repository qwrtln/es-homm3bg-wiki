## Notas

- La carta debe jugarse inmediatamente después de declarar el ataque, antes de que se jueguen otras cartas.
- El resultado del ataque de ataque se niega, * así como * todos los efectos futuros que aumentan el: Ataque: de la unidad de ataque.
- Misfortune can not be played to negate effects of cards that were already played and have already increased the :attack: of the attacking unit. The players should mutually agree if they can no longer play Misfortune after the other effects were played, or if the opponent gets to take his played :attack:-enhancing cards back into their hand.
