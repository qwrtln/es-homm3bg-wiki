## Notas

- **Manada** - Should the Pit Lords use their [alternative action](../keywords/alternative_action.md), they are not allowed to move, attack or defend.
- **Pack** - Should the Pit Lords summon [Pocos Demons](demons.md), they have to be placed adjacent to the Pit Lords.
- **Pack** - Should the Pit Lords reinforce [Pocos Demons](demons.md) to a [Pack of Demons](demons.md), there is no recruitment cost to be paid.
- **Manada** - The summoned [Demonios](demons.md) will activate during the Combat round when they were summoned, as long as their initiative is lower or equal to the initiative of the Pit Lords. If the initiative is higher, they are treated as if they were already activated during this Combat round.
- **Manada** - The summoned or reinforced [Demonios](demons.md) stay in the player's army after the end of the Combat.
- **Manada** - The Pit Lords may only summon or reinforce [Demonios](demons.md) once per Combat.
