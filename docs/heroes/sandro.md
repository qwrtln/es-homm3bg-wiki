## Notas

- **Ⅰ and Ⅳ** - this can only be played on a *pack* of [Skeletons](../units/skeletons.md) or [Zombies](../units/zombies.md), adding another amount to them, named "Horde".
- **Ⅳ** - the abilities of the [Manada de Zombies](../units/zombies.md) become inactive if they are upgraded to a "Horde". Once the "Horde" is defeated and they become a "Manada" again, their abilities function as usual.
- **Ⅵ** - can be played on "Pocos", "Pack", or even "Horde" of [Esqueletos](../units/skeletons.md).
- [^1] **Ⅵ** - If the [Esqueletos](../units/skeletons.md) are upgraded to a "Legion", they may be further upgraded to a "Manada" or a "Horde", but the "Legion" stays on top and its statistics are applied until defeated.
