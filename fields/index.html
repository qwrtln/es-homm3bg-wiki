    Lugares¶

    Fields are single cells on a tile. When your Hero moves to a field, they must immediately visit it, or first start a Combat against the enemies guarding it before visiting. Roman numerals written on a field indicate that the field is guarded by Neutral enemies that must be fought to visit it.

    Visitable Field¶

    Once you visit this field, place a Black Cube on it. Treat it as an Empty Field as long as it has a Black Cube.

    Revisitable Field¶

    You can visit this field multiple times. Do not place any Cubes when you visit it. You may pay 1 MP to fisit this field again.

    Flaggable Field¶

    These fields can be captured by players and provide passive benefits. When you visit one, place your Faction Cube on it. Enemy Heroes who visit your flagged fields will replace your Cube with theirs to steal the field’s effects. Allied Heroes treat flagged fields as if they were empty.

    Random Town Flaggable Field When revealed, all players roll 2 Resource dice. The highest roller chooses an unused Faction. The random Town is defended by Units from that Faction. They have a "Pack" of <bronze>, two "Packs" of <silver>, and two "Fews" of <golden> Units. The <bronze> Unit is chosen by the player who controls the Units during that Combat.

    When flagged, increases <gold> income by 10. The first player to flag the Town also gains its income immediately.
    Redwood Observatory Visitable Field Discover a face down Tile adjacent to this one.
    Resource Symbol Visitable Field Roll a specified number of Resource dice, then select one to resolve its effect.
    Sanctuary Revisitable Field Heroes on this Field cannot be attacked by other Heroes. Friendly Heroes can move through enemy Heroes on this Field but cannot stop here.
    Scholar Visitable Field Roll 1 Attack Die. Depending on the result, do the following:

    +1 - Gain a Statistic Card of your choice or Remove a Statistic Card from your hand.

    0 - Draw 2 Cards from the Ability Deck, gain one of them and discard the other.

    -1 - Draw 2 Cards from the Spell Deck, gain one of them and discard the other.
    Sea Barrel Visitable Field Roll and resolve 1 <resource_die>.
    Sea Chest Visitable Field Roll 1 Attack Die. Depending on the result, do the following:

    +1 - Search(1) <artifact>.

    0 - Gain 5 <gold>.

    -1 - Gain nothing.
    Settlement Flaggable Field Works as a spawn point for Secondary Heroes or Main Heroes that have been defeated.

    Works the same way as a mine, but the player can choose which resource income to increase. The settlement is then marked with a token of the chosen resource.

    — OR —
